About the Artist

The story behind Everlasting Memories and the passion that drives every design.

Isabella Garcia

Balloon Artist & Event Decorator · Founder, Everlasting Memories

Isabella Garcia founded Everlasting Memories in 2019 with a simple mission: transform ordinary celebrations into unforgettable visual experiences. What began as a side passion for crafting balloon bouquets for family birthdays has grown into a full-service event decoration studio serving clients across Connecticut.

Over the past 5+ years, Isabella has personally designed and installed decor for over 200 events—ranging from intimate baby showers to 500-guest corporate galas. Her work has earned a 97% client satisfaction rate and a loyal base of 50+ repeat clients who return for every milestone.

What Sets Her Apart?

Isabella specializes in neutral, earthy, and blush-toned palettes that photograph beautifully and complement modern event aesthetics. Unlike mass-produced decor rentals, every piece is handcrafted on-site with attention to color matching, spatial proportion, and structural integrity.
