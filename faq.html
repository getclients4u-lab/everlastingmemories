Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking balloon decor for your Connecticut event.

Balloon garlands at Everlasting Memories start at $250 for a 6-foot organic garland. Larger installations (12+ feet), complex color blends, or premium finishes (chrome, confetti, LED accents) require custom quotes. Pricing includes design, materials, delivery, and on-site installation.

We recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance for standard events and 8-12 weeks for weddings and large corporate galas. Last-minute bookings (under 2 weeks) may be accommodated based on availability, subject to a rush fee of 20%.

Everlasting Memories serves Stamford, New Haven, Hartford, Bridgeport, Greenwich, Norwalk, Westport, Darien, New Canaan, Fairfield, Milford, and surrounding Connecticut areas. Events outside these regions may incur a travel fee based on mileage.

With hi-float treatment, indoor latex balloons last 48-72 hours. Air-filled designs (garlands, backdrops) can last 1-2 weeks indoors. Outdoor installations are weather-dependent and typically designed for same-day events. We avoid outdoor setups in direct sunlight, rain, or temperatures below 50°F.
