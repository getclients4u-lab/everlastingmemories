Balloon Garlands

Balloon garlands are the most requested event decoration in 2026, accounting for an estimated 45% of all custom balloon decor bookings nationwide. At Everlasting Memories, we handcraft each garland to your exact color palette, venue dimensions, and aesthetic vision.

What Are Balloon Garlands?

A balloon garland is a connected arrangement of balloons in varying sizes (typically 5" to 18") that creates a flowing, organic or structured visual line. Unlike traditional balloon arches, garlands offer asymmetrical, free-form designs that feel modern and artful.

Organic Garlands
Asymmetrical clusters with varying balloon sizes, mimicking natural shapes. Best for boho, rustic, and modern events.
Structured Garlands
Uniform patterns with consistent spacing and sizing. Ideal for corporate events, product launches, and formal celebrations.
Half-Arch Garlands
A flat-backed garland designed to frame a doorway, dessert table, or stage without full 360° coverage.

Pricing starts at $250 for a 6-foot organic garland. Larger installations (12+ feet) and complex color blends require custom quotes.

Backdrops

A well-designed backdrop increases guest photo sharing by an estimated 340%, according to event marketing studies. Our backdrops serve as the visual anchor of your event—the spot where memories are captured and shared.

How Much Does a Custom Backdrop Cost?

Custom backdrops range from $400 to $1,500+ depending on size, materials, and complexity. A standard 8×8 ft balloon-and-fabric backdrop starts at $550. Add-ons like custom signage, floral accents, or LED lighting adjust the final price.

Backdrop Styles We Offer

Balloon Wall
Full-coverage balloon surface in organic or grid patterns. Maximum visual impact for photo booths and head tables.
Mixed Media
Balloons combined with fabric drapes, pampas grass, dried florals, or neon signage for a layered, editorial look.
Statement Arch
A freestanding arch framing a specific area—cake table, sweetheart table, or stage entrance.
Branded Backdrop
Corporate logos, hashtags, or product names integrated into the design for marketing activations.
